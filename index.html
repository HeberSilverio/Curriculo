About Me

I am a student and Undergraduate Research Assistant at UNC Charlotte.

My work centers around developing visually engaging learning tools for early computer science students to reduce attrition rates in CS fields.

I'm responsible for developing newer branches of the Bridges API to support a more robust curriculum.

The newest branch aims to support the core logic of fundamental algorithms and A.I. through scaffolding and abstracting advanced code; giving students more room to explore these concepts as early as their first programming course.

I'm very passionate about developing technology which has a positive impact — especially where it assists those who are working toward self-improvement.
